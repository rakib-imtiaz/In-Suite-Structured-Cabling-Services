## üé® **VISUAL STYLE GUIDELINES**

### **Color Palette:**
- **Primary Colors:** Match website (Deep Blue #0B5394, Teal #00A8A8)
- **Overall Tone:** Professional, clean, modern
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, warm tones
- **Contrast:** High contrast for clarity
- **Saturation:** Slightly desaturated for professional feel (85-90%)

### **Cinematography:**
- **Frame Rate:** 24fps (cinematic) or 30fps (smooth)
- **Shutter Speed:** 1/48 or 1/60 (natural motion blur)
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 - f/4 (shallow depth of field for focus)
- **ISO:** Keep low (100-400) for clean image
- **Stabilization:** Use gimbal or tripod - NO shaky footage

### **Camera Movements:**
- **Slow Push-In:** Creates focus and importance
- **Gentle Dolly:** Smooth horizontal movement
- **Slow Pull-Back:** Reveals context
- **Static with Motion:** Subject moves, camera still
- **NO:** Fast pans, quick zooms, shaky handheld

### **Composition:**
- **Rule of Thirds:** Always
- **Leading Lines:** Use architecture and cables
- **Symmetry:** Show organization and precision
- **Negative Space:** Clean, uncluttered frames
- **Depth:** Foreground, midground, background layers

- **Style:** Modern, professional, subtle
- **Genre:** Ambient electronic, modern corporate, or instrumental

### **Audio Levels:**
- **Music:** -12dB to -18dB (background)

- **Peak Levels:** -3dB maximum
- **Overall:** Professional, polished mix
